Cancellation and Refund Policy

Last updated: 27 May 2026

SpareHub handles cancellations and refunds based on order status, operational feasibility, and product-specific conditions.

Order cancellation

Customers may request order cancellation before dispatch or before the order enters a non-reversible fulfillment stage. Cancellation approval is subject to order processing status.

Refund eligibility

Refunds may be considered in cases such as successful cancellation prior to dispatch, duplicate payment, payment failure with debit, or verified fulfillment issues reviewed by SpareHub.

Non-refundable situations

Refunds may not be available for completed deliveries, custom orders, customer-side input mistakes, or cases outside SpareHub's verified responsibility, unless otherwise required by law.

Refund timeline

Approved refunds are generally initiated to the original payment source. Final credit timelines depend on the payment gateway, banking network, and the customer's financial institution.

Support for disputes

Customers may contact SpareHub support with order details, payment information, and issue summary for cancellation or refund review.
