Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 May 2026

SpareHub values customer privacy and is committed to protecting personal information collected through the SpareHub mobile application and related business communication channels.

Information we collect

We may collect customer information such as name, mobile number, email address, delivery address, order details, and payment-related order references required to process purchases and support customers.

How we use information

  • To process spare parts orders and coordinate delivery.
  • To communicate order updates, receipts, and support responses.
  • To improve app performance, product catalog quality, and service operations.

Payment information

Payments are processed through Razorpay. SpareHub does not store complete card or sensitive banking credentials on its own systems.

Data sharing

We may share limited information only with payment processors, delivery partners, and service providers as required to fulfill customer orders and maintain platform operations.

Data security

SpareHub takes reasonable technical and operational measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

Contact for privacy concerns

For privacy-related questions, please contact us using the details on the Contact Us page.
