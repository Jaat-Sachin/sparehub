Shipping Policy

Last updated: 27 May 2026

SpareHub processes and coordinates delivery of spare parts based on order confirmation, product availability, and serviceable delivery areas.

Processing time

Orders are typically processed after payment confirmation and internal order review. Processing time may vary depending on product availability and operational requirements.

Delivery timelines

Delivery timelines may vary by destination, product type, and shipping method. Estimated delivery timelines, if provided, are indicative only.

Shipping charges

Shipping charges, if applicable, will be shown or communicated during the order flow or prior to final confirmation.

Delivery limitations

SpareHub reserves the right to limit or decline shipping to certain locations depending on logistics feasibility, compliance requirements, or other operational constraints.

Customer responsibility

Customers are responsible for providing accurate delivery information and being available to receive the shipment or coordinate receipt.
